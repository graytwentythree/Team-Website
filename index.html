Welcome

We are the Sunseeker Solar Car Project, Sunseeker for short. We are a team of students from Western Michigan University in Kalamazoo, Michigan that designs, builds, and races our solar powered vehicle in the American Solar Challenge and Formula Sun Grand Prix. If you would like to learn more, you can find more about our team on the about us page.

If you would like to get in touch with the team, you can send an email to sunseeker@wmich.edu.
